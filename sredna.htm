Driving under the influence of alcohol

 Disrespect for traffic signs
Every year there are traffic accidents due to "one glass" of fun that will end fatally. These events will continue to happen if the human conscience does not start working. Accidents are most common among young people who are not very experienced with their vehicle. and think that their actions will have no consequences. In Macedonia, traffic signs are underestimated because people's thinking is that if they don't see you, you won't be held accountable for your irresponsibility. Unfortunately, the signs are not in good condition, the targets are broken or sprayed with graphite..That's why we need to be careful because you may respect traffic rules and regulations, but you can also be hurt by someone else's mistakes.
